Overview

Foodie. ("we", "us", or "our") is a restaurant discovery and journaling app built to help you find, save, and remember great restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Foodie.:

  • Account information — your email address, display name, username, and profile photo when you create an account
  • Food preferences — favorite cuisine, spice tolerance, dietary preferences, and other preferences you choose to share
  • Restaurant data — restaurants you save, personal ratings and reviews you write, and photos you upload from your visits
  • Location data — your approximate location when you use the Discover feature, used only to find nearby restaurants
  • Usage data — basic app interactions to help us improve the experience

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core Foodie. service — saving restaurants, showing recommendations, and displaying your journal
  • To authenticate your account securely via Apple Sign In or Google Sign In
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

Third Party Services

Foodie. uses the following third party services to operate:

  • Supabase — securely stores your account information, saved restaurants, reviews, and photos
  • Yelp Fusion API — provides restaurant search results and business information
  • Apple Sign In — optional authentication method
  • Google Sign In — optional authentication method

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Photos you upload are stored in secure cloud storage. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Your Rights

  • Access — you can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Profile tab in the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Export — contact us at hritish@hpfoodie.com to request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

Foodie. is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
